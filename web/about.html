Welcome

Welcome to Studley College, sited in the centre of the rural village of Studley in the county of Warwickshire. Our college specialises in Technology, Art & Design and has recently embarked on a new exciting expansion programme by opening a new Campus in January 2017 in Astwood Bank, Worcestershire.

We are extremely proud of our hi-technology facilities, which offer students the opportunity to study using cutting edge technolgies in the world of design. High quality and excellent standards of teaching and learning attribute to the success of the college and its overall Outstanding Ofsted judgement (2013 - 2014).

The College offers students the opportunity to study in a safe environment, providing outstanding support for all of our students, through personal tutorial systems, a student welfare office and counselling service.
