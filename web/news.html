New Campus

WED 4 JAN

Studley College is proud to announce the opening of its Astwood Bank Campus specialising in IT.

The new Astwood Bank campus allows more students than ever to study with Studley College. This new advancement allows an extra 1000 students from the local area, as well as 200 additional international places to appeal to international student targets.

The new campus will contain state of the art IT resources to give students the best education and the funding to explore new courses.

On Wednesday the 4th of January, the new site will have its official opening at 11:00am and welcome new students to come and explore our new campus and the courses it will offer.
